# Image sources — On the Footsteps of Lord Buddha

(jpeg q82–84). **Pexels** (no attribution required; photographer credited as
courtesy) covers Delhi, Lucknow, Bodhgaya, Varanasi, the hero and the hotels.

Lumbini peace pagoda, Kushinagar, Vaishali) are from **Wikimedia Commons**
(free licenses listed below). Hotel images are **representative** luxury
stand-ins (free-licensed photos of the exact named property weren't available)
